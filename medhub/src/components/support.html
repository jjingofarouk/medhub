Frequently Asked Questions (FAQs)

How do I reset my password?

To reset your password, go to the login page and click on "Forgot Password." Follow the instructions to reset your password.

How can I update my profile?

To update your profile, log in to your account and go to the "Profile" page. You can update your information there.

What should I do if I encounter a bug?

If you encounter a bug, please contact our support team with details about the issue and steps to reproduce it. We will address it as soon as possible.
